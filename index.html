About Me

Hi, I am Lizhi Wang, a joint Ph.D. student at School of Electronic Engineering of Xidian University and Internet Media Group at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA). I am now working at MSRA, Beijing, co-supervised by Dr. Zhiwei Xiong (MSRA), Prof. Feng Wu (USTC) and Prof. Guangming Shi (Xidian University). My major research interests focus on Computational photography, Computer vision, Sparse representation, and Hyperspectral imaging.

Contact Me : lzwang@stu.xidian.edu.cn
