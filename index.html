About Me

I am an associate professor with school of computer science & technology, Beijing Institute of technology (BIT). I received the B.S. degree and the Ph.D degree from Xidian University in 2011 and 2016, respectively. I attended the joint Ph.D. program of Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA). I am now a member of the Visual Media Computing Lab at BIT.

Email : lzwang@bit.edu.cn
